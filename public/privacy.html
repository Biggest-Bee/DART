Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 20, 2026
1. Introduction

DART ("we", "our", or "the Service") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our file storage and sharing service.

By using DART, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Your email address, display name, and user ID when you sign in with Google or create an account.
  • File Data: The files you upload, including their content, names, sizes, and metadata.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the Service, including upload history, file access patterns, and interactions.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and device identifiers for analytics and service improvement.
3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Store and manage your files securely
  • Enable file sharing and publishing features
  • Authenticate users and prevent unauthorized access
  • Communicate with you about the Service
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve performance
4. Data Storage and Security
📁 File Storage
Your files are stored on Google Cloud Storage, a secure, scalable cloud storage service provided by Google. Files are encrypted at rest using industry-standard encryption methods. Storage locations are determined by your Firebase project configuration and may include data centers in the United States or other regions based on your settings.
🔐 Password and Authentication
Important: DART does not store your passwords directly. When you sign in with Google, authentication is handled by Google OAuth 2.0. We receive a secure token from Google that confirms your identity without ever accessing or storing your Google password. For anonymous sign-in, we use Firebase Authentication which generates secure, temporary credentials without requiring passwords.
🗄️ Metadata Storage
File metadata (names, sizes, upload dates, sharing settings) is stored in Google Cloud Firestore, a NoSQL cloud database. This data is encrypted and protected by Google's security infrastructure.
⚡ Real-time Data
Upload progress and live activity data is temporarily stored in Google Firebase Realtime Database for real-time synchronization. This data is not permanently retained.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption of data at rest
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates
5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data or files to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly choose to share files with other users or publish files publicly.
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers (Google Cloud, Firebase) who assist in operating the Service.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.
6. Data Retention

We retain your files and account data for as long as you maintain your account. If you delete your account or specific files, we will delete the associated data from our servers within a reasonable time frame, subject to backup and recovery policies.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, download, or delete your files at any time
  • Update your account information
  • Revoke sharing permissions for files you've shared
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of certain data collection (where applicable)
8. Children's Privacy

DART is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, particularly where Google Cloud/Firebase data centers are located. These transfers are conducted in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the DART application or by email.
